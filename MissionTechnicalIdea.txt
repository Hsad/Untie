types of actions:
	take (steal, pick up supplies)
	leave (lay incrimating evidence, place bomb)
	change state (turn on power, break car, assassinate)

take actions add objects to the groups posession
leave remove objects
change state acts upon objects in the world

locations are where objects exist
objects can exist in diffrent locations at diffrent times

missions involve goals and requirements
goals are the stated action to preform on an object

requirements are the presence, or absence, of objects in particular states
